Focus Flare Production Policy

Refund Policy

Refunds can be given within the first 30 days. If Client(s) are not satisfied with the first rough cut or final cut within the first 30 days of the deliverable they have the right to request a refund, UNLESS the late fee policy has already been enacted.

Cancellations

There will be a $60 fee for any shoot cancelled with less than 24 hour notice and an $85 fee for any shoot cancelled on site without notice.

Travel Fees

Travel fees will be charged if outside of the 30 mile radius of service area.

Late Fees

Late Fees will only be activated if a Client misses a payment when payment is due. Once Late Fees are activated Client understands that there is no refund. Late Fees are as followed: there is a $20 fee per week if Client fails to deliver payment promptly. An additional $10 flat fee may be charged if Client fails to provide the necessary materials to Focus Flare Productions such as power point slides for a lecture series or any other required content and a sign contract. There is NO REFUNDS once the late fee is activated.

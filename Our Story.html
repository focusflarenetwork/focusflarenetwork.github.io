A Few Words About Us

Joe Finnegan
Owner and Founder

Joe is the founder and owner of Focus Flare Productions. Focus Flare Productions started in 2016. Joe graduated from Fairliegh Dickinson Univeristy with his bacholers in Film and a concentration in lighting. Joe's senior thesis "Rabidus" was chosen for offical selection at the NY Upstate Horror Film Festival and also won honorable mention.Joe has worked on several big name movies and TV shows such as "A Case of Blue" and "Ray Donovan Season 7".

Focus Flare Productions provides local businesses with promotional videos, commercials and testimonials for their company. Focus Flare Productions also provides videos for all events including; wedding, music videos, real estate listings, charity and fundraising events, sport analysis videos and corporate videos. Please contact us to see how our creativity, enthusiasim and commitment can help your business grow and how we can make your special event memorable for years to come.
